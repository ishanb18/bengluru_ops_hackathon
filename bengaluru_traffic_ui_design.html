Heavy Vehicle Breakdown Profiler
Your dataset has cargo_material, reason_breakdown, and age_of_truck for 276 truck breakdown events. Older trucks (avg age 15 years) break down more often — specifically "starting problems" and "tyre bursts". This feature profiles which truck types are highest-risk and flags old-truck corridors for pre-emptive patrolling. Also classifies cargo type (goods vs empty) to determine tow truck size needed. This is genuinely novel — no other team will think of truck age as a risk signal.

Planned Event Advance Planner
467 events are "planned" (construction, public events, VIP movements, processions). Your dataset captures them before they start. Build a calendar view showing upcoming planned events for the next 7 days — with pre-calculated manpower requirements, diversion routes, and officer deployment orders generated automatically before the event happens. This directly solves the "event impact not quantified in advance" gap from the problem statement. Construction events (311 planned) get flagged for BBMP coordination automatically.

Citizen Report Authenticity Scorer
12.3% of incidents (1,007 of 8,173) are marked "authenticated: no" — meaning citizen-reported events not yet verified by an officer. Right now these are just ignored. Build a credibility scorer that uses: incident cause plausibility, location (is it on a known high-risk corridor?), hour of day, and historical accuracy of that reporting source. Score 0–100. High-score unverified reports get auto-escalated for officer verification. This tackles the "reactive enforcement" problem directly — you catch real incidents faster before they become worse.
